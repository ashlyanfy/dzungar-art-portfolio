Love will go away along the thin plank of separation, wringing its hands. And you yourself have been watching it for a long time with calm eyes.
I’m Dzungar, an artist who focuses on simple but meaningful things. Mongolia inspires me with its landscapes and the sense of freedom that comes from the open steppe. In my art, I use humor and irony not as decoration but as a way to look at the world differently. I don’t aim to create perfect images; I aim to capture ideas, moments, and perspectives. My work is about freedom, simplicity, and sharing thoughts with those who can see meaning in small details
